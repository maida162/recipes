Pizza Ramen Recipe Ranking

🍕 Pizza

Pizza is probably my favorite food of all time. My mom makes it on Fridays sometimes and it's honestly way better than any delivery place. The secret is fresh mozzarella.

My go-to toppings:

  • Pepperoni (obviously)
  • Extra cheese
  • Mushrooms (my dad's influence)

Pineapple on pizza is a controversial topic in my friend group. I think it's fine. Tyler disagrees strongly.

🍜 Ramen

Real ramen from a restaurant is completely different from the instant noodle kind. There's a place near my house called Noodle House and their broth is incredible.

Types I've tried:

  • Tonkotsu — rich pork broth, very creamy
  • Shoyu — soy sauce based, lighter
  • Miso — my personal favorite
  • Instant cup noodles — still valid at midnight

📝 My Grilled Cheese Recipe

Something I actually know how to make. It's simple but really good.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (American or Cheddar)
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder (optional but recommended)

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of the bread
  2. Heat pan on medium
  3. Place bread in pan, add cheese
  4. Top with second slice
  5. Flip after 2–3 minutes
  6. Cook until both sides are golden brown
  7. Eat immediately

💡 Pro tip: add garlic powder to the butter. trust me on this one.
